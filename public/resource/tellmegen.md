## How to Contribute

[Contribute Now](https://www.tellmegen.com/en/tellmegen-research)

### Steps

1. Log in to your tellmeGen account
2. Go to 'Legal consent document' in your profile or settings
3. Check the box for 'Research with pseudonymized/anonymized data'
4. Complete surveys in your private area to provide health data
