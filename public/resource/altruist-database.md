# Altruist Database (Sequencing.com)

> A free database of anonymous genotypic, phenotypic and interpretation data that allows public searching and analysis. Researchers can request to contact individuals holding specific traits for additional paid studies and opportunities.

**Organizations:** Sequencing.com

**Persistent ID:** https://yourselftoscience.org/resource/5297c0c6-2633-4109-b7d6-2016045227af
**Canonical URL:** https://yourselftoscience.org/resource/altruist-database

## Details

- **Data Types:** Genome

- **Based In:** United States

- **Compatible Sources:** Raw Data, WGS, Sequencing.com, 23andMe, AncestryDNA, MyHeritage, FamilyTreeDNA, tellmeGen

## How to Contribute

[Contribute Now](https://sequencing.com/knowledge-center/altruist-endeavor/altruist-database)

### Steps

1. Upload your genetic data files into your Sequencing.com account
2. Navigate to your 'My Files' page
3. Change the 'Altruist' toggle button for your file from 'No' to 'Yes'
4. Enable 'Receive additional offers' in Settings > Altruism Rewards to receive incentives from researchers
