## How to Contribute

[Contribute Now](https://customercare.23andme.com/hc/en-us/articles/212195708-Research-Participation-and-Consent)

### Steps

1. Log in to your 23andMe account
2. Go to Account Settings
3. View/Change consent under Research Participation
4. Optionally review Individual Data Sharing Consent
