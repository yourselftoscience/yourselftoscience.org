## How to Contribute

[Contribute Now](https://www.myheritage.com/dna-informed-consent-agreement)

### Steps

1. Log in to your MyHeritage account
2. Go to 'Settings' > 'My Privacy' > 'My DNA preferences'
3. Enable 'MyHeritage DNA Research Project' consent
4. Complete surveys to assess health and other traits
